first post

Today I'm starting a blog.

I work at amazon robotics, generally as a dev and roboticist. My current focus is on robotic perception & task/action planning. I also do a fair amount of lower level (in terms of abstraction) dev for work; including configuring networks, writing services that interact with hardware, and other deeply frustrating things.

I plan on writing about things I'm interested. I aim to keep the posts / articles at roughly the length of my attention span. With the rise of short form content (tiktok and youtube shorts), I suspect that is for the best.

things i'm interested in:

  • manipulation
  • robotics, mechatronics, electronics
  • networking & middleware
  • perception
  • machine learning
  • general autonomy, agent dev, etc

I will try to write roughly once a week. It will be concerning if I don't have something useful to share at least once a week, as it indicates I'm not working on the right stuff on my free time.
